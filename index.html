FAQ

Stuff you might need to know

What is LexSciBowl?

LexSciBowl is a new middle school science competition run by members of the Lexington High School Science Bowl Team. Teams of up to 5 members will compete in both a written competition modeled on the US Science Olympiads and a buzzer competition modeled on the National Science Bowl. Team members and local scientists will also be giving talks on all sorts of science topics!

When and where?

We're holding LexSciBowl at Lexington High School in the Science Lecture Hall. Just show up at one of the entrances to the school between 8:00 and 8:30 on May 16, and signs and volunteers will direct you to the right place. (see the Schedule for more details)

How do I get there?

We can't really offer any sort of bus transportation ourselves, but we encourage you to use the MBTA public bus system.

How much does it cost?

The registration fee is $5, which does not include lunch. Bring your money as cash or check to Town of Lexington. This is a big fundraiser for the our school's Science Bowl team - thank you for participating!

How will I eat?

Lunch will be during Breaktime/Talktime, which is an hour and a half long and includes science talks! You have a few options for lunch:

  • You can pre-order pizzas for $12 each online before the competition, or for $15 each at the competition, or $2 per slice. All profits go toward running LexSciBowl.
  • You can go to Lexington Center, which is a few minutes' walk away.
  • Finally, you can always pack your own lunch.

What should I bring?

Typical test-taking implements (pencils, erasers) [calculators?]

How can I practice for the competition?

[scibowl ms rounds] [all questions written by us though so probs a bit harder :)] [uh what is easy for olympiads]

How is LexSciBowl run?

We're all student-run! A few members of the Science Bowl team decided it might be cool to bring our love of high school science bowl to middle schools in the region. If you represent a company that might like to sponsor us, we'd love to talk to you about it! Just contact us at sponsors@lexscibowl.org. (unfortunately we cannot accept private donations at this time)

How do I sign up?

Right here! There's a registration form at the bottom of this page. Key point - for this year, you need to sign up as a teacher-sponsored team, so see if your teacher will be willing to organize it!

That covers most of it. If you still have something to ask, please shoot us an email at contact@lexscibowl.org! We'll be happy to answer any questions you might have.
